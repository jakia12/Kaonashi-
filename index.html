How to Buy

1. Create a Wallet

Download phantom or your wallet of choice from the app store or google play store for free. Desktop users, download the google chrome extension by going to phantom.io

2. Deposit SOL

Deposit SOL into your phantom wallet to have funds for buying Kaonashi .

3.Go to Raydium

Connect to Raydium. Go to app.Raydium.org in google chrome or on the browser inside your phantomapp. Connect your wallet. Paste the Kaonashi token address into Raydium, select Pepe, and confirm when phantomprompts you for a wallet signature, and sign.

4.Swap SOL for Kaonashi

We have ZERO taxes so you don't need to worry about buying with a specific slippage, although you may need to use slippage during times of market volatility.
